## Part 4: Content Hub — Upload Once, Publish Everywhere

One place to upload photos and write a post, with a channel picker before publishing:

**☐ GBP Post  ☐ Blog  ☐ Newsletter  ☐ GBP Update (info/hours/services)**

### Photo uploads
- Central media library; every upload auto-resized to **1200×900 (4:3)** for GBP posts, 720×720 for GBP gallery photos, JPG/PNG, 10KB–5MB
- Real photos only — stock images underperform and can get GBP posts rejected
- Tag photos by client/job so the website gallery, GBP, and case studies pull from the same pool

### Rewritten snippet (replaces the old "review automation only" GBP line in Services → SEO & AI Search Optimization)

> **Google Business Profile Management — done to the current standard.**
> Your GBP is your real homepage for local search. We don't just claim it — we run it:
> - **Fresh posts every week** (2–3×/week in competitive markets), photos on every post, offer and event posts with CTAs and UTM tracking
> - **Photo uploads monthly minimum** — real job-site and product photos, properly sized, geo-relevant
> - **Profile completeness**: categories, services, products, attributes, Q&A seeding, hours/holiday updates
> - **Review engine**: automated requests, owner responses within 24h, keywords woven into replies
> - **One dashboard**: write it once, push it to your GBP, your blog, and your email newsletter at the same time — no logging into three tools

### How it's built (GHL stack you already run)
- **GHL Social Planner** connects directly to GBP — schedule and post without opening Google; supports recurring posts (Google is rolling recurring GBP posts out natively through 2026)
- **GHL blog + email builder** cover the Blog and Newsletter channels from the same content
- Practical workflow: upload photo → write 100-character hook first (GBP truncates at ~100 chars before "More") → pick channels → schedule. Blog version gets the long copy; newsletter gets the blog link; GBP gets hook + photo + CTA button
- For client accounts: each sub-account gets the same hub, so this becomes a **sellable monthly deliverable** ("we keep your Google profile alive every week")

### GBP optimization standard (2026)
| Item | Standard |
|---|---|
| Post frequency | 1×/week minimum; 2–3×/week competitive markets; >3×/week = diminishing returns |
| Post images | 1200×900 (4:3), min 400×300, real photos, in focus |
| Copy | Key info + CTA in first 100 characters; emojis roughly double clicks |
| Post types | Updates, Offers (with redemption window), Events (with date/time) |
| Tracking | UTM-tagged links on every post CTA |
| Photos to profile | Add new photos at least monthly, not just to posts |
